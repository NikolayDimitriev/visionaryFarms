Vision

About us, our values and mission

Visionary Farms is a young company that
develops optimised cultivation solutions for
growing crops in controlled environments.

We make smart cultivation technology
accessible to individuals, small businesses,
and medium-sized enterprises.

In developing our solutions, we prioritise
efficiency and productivity. This makes
smart cultivation appealing to everyone.

Success in controlled environment cultivation
requires knowledge in multiple complicated fields, including plant science, light engineering,
hydroponics, as well as software and automation.

Lacking knowledge in any of these areas makes it
challenging to achieve stable cultivation and predictable harvests. Therefore, our vision and philosophy revolve around sharing the knowledge required for success. We strive to make our systems and components efficient and productive, but also straightforward and user-friendly.

Our goal is to simplify the complex
and make it accessible.

Software that’s not hard.

The core of our solutions is our software system, available to users through our mobile app. The app has an intuitive interface, making Cloud-enabled automation simple and accessible. It is a living ecosystem that we develop in collaboration with our users.

Our key competencies.

The key ingredients to growing full of pleasure and profit.

Plant science

In the past, knowledge about growing specific varieties was only accessible to a few, and that expertise was never shared beyond large-scale agricultural operations.

We digitize the expertise of plant scientists by translating their knowledge into algorithms for lighting, hydroponic and climate control systems. Once digitized, we adjust and refine these algorithms and finally share them with our users.

Our goal is to ensure that our technology provides a stable and high yield, minimising crop loss and maximising pleasure.

Lighting

Lighting enables us to replace the sun and grow vertically, achieving a high crop density, regardless of the location on the planet. However, lighting is the main investment component and a significant portion of operating expenses related to the electricity consumption of farms. The efficiency and cost-effectiveness of the lighting solutions used have a huge impact on the length of the payback period.

Light engineering is a key competency of the Visionary Farms team, with experience in creating lighting fixtures that have sold hundreds of thousands of units and lighting installations on a city scale. We put this knowledge towards creating efficient and easy-to-use cultivation lighting to suit your needs.

Automation and IT

Our cloud-based software system orchestrates all of the elements of cultivation, automatically creating and maintaining the optimal conditions for your crops.

You interact with the software system through our mobile app, where you can manually set cultivation parameters or access our cultivation recipes. Visionary Farms algorithms are constantly updated and refined to optimise your cultivation.

Visionary Farms R&D centre

Our R&D centre is located in a repurposed basement boiler room of an office building.

The centre uses our hardware and software for developing new cultivation algorithms, as well as research and development in the following areas:

Using micropropagation for large-scale production of strawberry seedlings.
Development of environmentally neutral synthetic biodegradable substrates for growing plants in vertical farms.
Optimization of lighting recipes for strawberry cultivation in environments completely deprived of natural light.
