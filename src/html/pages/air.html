Air senses conditions.

Air continually senses the ambient temperature, relative humidity and CO2 concentration in your grow room.

Data uploads to the cloud.

All information gathered by Air automatically uploads to the cloud and can be accessed via the Visionary Farms app. Reports and analytics are produced, giving you live updates on the conditions of your farm.

Automatic adjustment.

Air checks sensor data against the specific parameters you set, and if they differ from the sensor readings, it automatically adjusts the workings of your farm to create the correct conditions.

Cloud subscription.

Whilst you can set parameters manually without the cloud subscription, using it unlocks Air's full potential and maximises the capabilities of your farm.
