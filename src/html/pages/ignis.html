  • Variable light spectrum for all plants needs

  • Optic system bends light for efficiency

Light spectra.

The light spectrum your plants are exposed to hugely impacts their growth. We alter the spectral combinations depending on the plant you are growing and its growth stage.

  • Universal Ignis
  • Ignis Control
  • Modified Ignis

Universal Ignis

The optimal ratio of the main PAR ranges achieves maximum produce yields.

Effective for

Most crops, including microgreens, leafy greens, edible flowers and strawberries, during all stages of growth.

Default

The Universal spectrum ships as the default for Ignis light modules due to itâs versatility.

Other Spectra

Equipping the Control Module enables the adjustment of the spectral combination through 3-channel control. Alternatively, other spectra are available when purchasing a modified version of Ignis. Please contact us to learn more.
